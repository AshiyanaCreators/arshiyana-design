About Vastu Shastra

Our website and services may include suggestions, advice, or design recommendations based on principles of Vastu Shastra, an ancient Indian architectural system. These suggestions are provided to enhance the aesthetic, spatial, and energetic harmony of spaces, in alignment with traditional beliefs.

Important Notice

Vastu-based advice is subjective and should not be interpreted as a guaranteed solution for health, wealth, or success. We do not claim that following Vastu guidelines will result in specific outcomes.

Nature of Vastu Recommendations

The effectiveness of Vastu depends on multiple factors, including personal beliefs and environmental conditions. Our Vastu suggestions are meant to complement modern architectural and design principles, creating spaces that feel harmonious and balanced according to traditional Indian wisdom.

Professional Consultation

Users are encouraged to consult professional architects, engineers, or planners when making critical structural decisions. While we incorporate Vastu principles into our design recommendations, all structural and safety considerations must be evaluated by qualified professionals.

Our Approach

At Ashiyana Design World, we respect and honor traditional Vastu principles while maintaining a practical approach to modern living. Our designs aim to create spaces that are both functionally efficient and spiritually harmonious, blending ancient wisdom with contemporary needs.

Scope of Application

Vastu recommendations may be applied to various aspects of design including:

  • Room placement and orientation
  • Color schemes and material selection
  • Furniture arrangement and spatial flow
  • Natural light and ventilation considerations
  • Garden and landscape design elements

Individual Responsibility

Clients choosing to incorporate Vastu principles into their projects do so at their own discretion. We provide these recommendations as an additional service to enhance the overall design experience, but the decision to implement them remains entirely with the client.

No Guarantees

We make no warranties or guarantees regarding the outcomes of implementing Vastu principles in design projects. Results may vary based on individual circumstances, beliefs, and environmental factors beyond our control.
